Privacy Policy

Effective June 1, 2015

Our privacy policy explains how information is collected, used and disclosed by Snippet with respect to your access and use of Snippit’s services, whether through our website (located at www.snippit.space), Snippit’s mobile applications or in connection with social networking services (referred to below as “Snippit”).

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect certain information you provide to us when using Snippit, such as when you sign up, or post information or other content to Snippit. We collect certain personal information, like your name, pictures and email address, as well as certain non-identifying information, like your gender, birthday and zip code. We will also collect your login credentials to Facebook.

We automatically collect certain information when you use Snippit, such as a device identifier (but not the UDID), MAC address, wireless service provider, Internet Protocol (IP) address (if using a browser), operating system, the browser type, the address of a referring site and your activity on Snippit. You can enable or disable location services when you use Snippit at anytime, through your mobile device settings. This information is not treated as personal information unless we combine it with or link it to any of the personally identifiable information mentioned above.

We may also automatically collect certain information through the use of “cookies”. These are small files that your browser places on your computer. We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our services, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on our services, and to improve our services. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by editing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.

We may use information collected through one Snippit service for other Snippit services that you use – for example, to present favorites on friends list.

ACCESSING AND MODIFYING YOUR INFORMATION

If you have an account, you can access and modify the personal information you provided us that is associated with your account. You may "opt out" of receiving marketing or promotional emails from us by changing your account preferences or by following the instructions in those emails. If you opt out of these messages, you will still receive emails from us, about your account, our services and other pertinent information related to our services. These communications are considered part of the service and your account, which you cannot opt-out from receiving.

You can deactivate your account by selecting the “deactivate” link. Deactivating your account lets you remove your profile without permanently deleting it. When you deactivate your account, other users will be able to see Snippits you sent in the past but your profile page will be blocked. If you want to delete your information and your account, please contact us with a request that we delete your account and information. We will take steps to delete your information as soon as is practicable, but some information may remain in archived/backup copies for our records and as otherwise required by law.
